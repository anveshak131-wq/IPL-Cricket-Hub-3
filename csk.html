About CSK

Chennai Super Kings is a professional cricket franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The team was founded in 2008 and plays in the Indian Premier League. Known as one of the most successful teams in IPL history, CSK has won 5 IPL championships (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023). Led by legendary captain MS Dhoni, the team is known for its consistency and loyal fan base.
