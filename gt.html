About the Team

Gujarat Titans is a franchise cricket team based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, that plays in the Indian Premier League. The team was founded in 2021 and made their debut in the 2022 season.

In a remarkable debut season, Gujarat Titans won the IPL championship in 2022, becoming only the third team to win the tournament in their maiden season. They achieved this feat under the leadership of Hardik Pandya.

The team plays their home matches at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, the largest cricket stadium in the world with a capacity of 132,000. The Titans are owned by CVC Capital Partners and have quickly established themselves as one of the most exciting teams in the IPL.
